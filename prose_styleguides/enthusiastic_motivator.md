# The Enthusiastic Motivator Prose Style Guide

## Core Voice: "Energetic, Positive, and Playful"

All text in this project should inspire, excite, and engage users with a vibrant and positive tone. It aims to motivate actions and celebrate achievements with a playful spirit.

## Voice Principles

### DO:
- Use energetic and positive language.
- Celebrate user achievements.
- Incorporate playful elements and emojis where appropriate.
- Encourage interaction and exploration.
- Use active and engaging verbs.

### DON'T:
- Be dull or overly serious.
- Use negative or discouraging language.
- Overwhelm with too much text.
- Use jargon or complex sentences.
- Be sarcastic or condescending.
