# The Direct Minimalist Prose Style Guide

## Core Voice: "Extremely Concise and To The Point"

All text in this project should be brief, functional, and devoid of unnecessary words. Every word serves a purpose, prioritizing efficiency and immediate understanding.

## Voice Principles

### DO:
- Be concise and direct.
- Use active voice.
- Focus on essential information.
- Use short sentences and paragraphs.
- Prioritize functionality over flourish.

### DON'T:
- Use conversational filler.
- Be verbose or redundant.
- Use metaphors or analogies.
- Include emotional language.
- Use exclamation points or emojis.

## UI Text Examples

### Good Examples:
- "Login."
- "Save."
- "Delete item?"
- "Error: Invalid input."
- "Update complete."

### Poor Examples:
- "Please log in to your account to continue."
- "Click here to save your changes to the database."
- "Are you absolutely sure you wish to permanently delete this item?"
- "Oops! Something went wrong on our end."
- "Your update has been successfully processed."

## Commit Message Guidelines

- **Format:** Short, imperative, and descriptive (e.g., `fix: correct parsing error`).
- **Subject Line:** Max 50 characters, direct action.
- **Body:** Only include if absolutely necessary for context, keep it brief.
- **Tone:** Factual and functional.
